What is Container Gardening?

What is Container Gardening? Container gardening is a vibrant and flexible approach to cultivating plants where traditional garden space is limited or unavailable. It involves growing plants in containers instead of planting them in the ground. This method suits a wide variety of plants, from herbs to small trees, and offers numerous benefits, including space efficiency, adaptability, and enhanced control over the growing environment. Benefits Space-Saving For those with limited outdoor space, such as apartment dwellers or homes without yards, container gardening is a game-changer.
