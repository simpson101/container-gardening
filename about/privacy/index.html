Privacy Policy for Container Gardens

Effective Date: 2024-03-15

At Container Gardens, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect from our users, how we use it, and the steps we take to ensure your personal information is handled securely.

Information Collection

  1. Personal Information: We collect personal information when you sign up for our services, place an order, or interact with our customer service. This information may include your name, email address, postal address, and payment details.

  2. Non-Personal Information: We automatically collect non-personal information when you visit our website, such as your IP address, browser type, and access times. This information is used to improve our website and services.

Use of Information

Your information is used to process orders, deliver products and services, process payments, communicate with you about orders and services, and enhance your experience with Container Gardens.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except as described in this policy. We may share information with service providers who assist us in our operations, under strict confidentiality agreements. Additionally, we may disclose information when required by law.

Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. Access to your personal information is restricted to employees and service providers who need to know that information to provide services to you.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience and gather information about visitors and visits to our websites.

Your Choices

You can choose not to provide personal information, although it might affect your ability to use certain services. You can also opt out of receiving promotional emails from us by following the unsubscribe instructions included in those emails.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page. You are advised to review this privacy policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.

By using our website, you consent to our privacy policy.
